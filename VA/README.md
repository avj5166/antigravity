## 💡 THE DAILY ROUTINE (5 Minutes Every Morning)

1. Open Google Sheet → Sort Deadlines tab by "Days Until" column
2. Check for any student marked "Urgent" or "At Risk"
3. Send any pending WhatsApp updates (scripts in Document 04)
4. Update Notion pages for any students who moved to a new stage

**That's it. The system does the rest.**
