## THE PARENT PITCH (When Parents Accompany the Student)

Parents have different concerns. They care about:
- **Safety of their money** (will this consultancy disappear?)
- **Visa success rate** (will my child actually get the visa?)
- **Transparency** (where is my money going? what is happening?)
- **Communication** (will you keep us informed?)

Adapt the pitch:

*"Mr./Mrs. [Name], I understand you want to make sure this is the right decision for [Student's name].*

*Let me tell you the three things that set us apart from every other consultancy you may have visited:*

*One — Transparency. We create a personal tracking portal for your child, which you can also see. Every document, every stage, every deadline — visible to you at all times. You don't need to wonder what's happening. You'll know.*

*Two — Specialization. We focus exclusively on France. Not 15 countries, not everything to everyone. France. We know every nuance of the Campus France process, the VFS requirements, the university application system. This depth is what gets visas approved.*

*Three — Documentation discipline. The #1 reason for France visa rejection is financial document issues. We review every financial document before it goes to VFS — and we flag problems early. Your money spent on this process is protected by our systematic review.*

*We are a small, focused consultancy — and that's our strength. Your child will not be one of 200 students we've forgotten about. We know every student by name and by stage.*"*

## YOUR PRICING STRATEGY (How to Price When You Have Competition)

### Don't compete on price. Compete on confidence.

Most Ahmedabad consultancies charge ₹40,000–80,000 for France visa assistance.

**Your positioning:** You're not the cheapest. You're the most thorough.

**Suggested Pricing:**

| Service | Price | What's Included |
|---|---|---|
| **Complete France Package** | ₹65,000–75,000 | Profile assessment, university shortlisting, SOP, LOR guidance, Campus France (all stages), document review, visa preparation, VFS guidance, pre-departure briefing |
| **Visa-Only Package** (student already has admission) | ₹25,000–35,000 | Document checklist, verification, VFS guidance only |
| **University Application Only** | ₹20,000–30,000 | SOP, shortlisting, applications — no visa |

**How to justify your price:**
*"Our complete package includes something no other consultancy in Ahmedabad offers — a dedicated personal portal where you and your parents can see every document, every stage, every deadline in real time. Plus WhatsApp updates at every milestone. You are never in the dark."*

**Payment Structure:**
- 40% on enrollment (signing agreement)
- 40% when Campus France attestation received
- 20% when visa is approved

This aligns your incentive with the student's success. They pay the most only when they succeed.
